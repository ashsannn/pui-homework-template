Ecuadorian Lentil Soup with 30g Protein

3 servings

300 calories/serving

serving size: 1 cup

50 mins | prep: 15 mins cook: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 3 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 4 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse lentils thoroughly under running water.
  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add the diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, and tomato to the pot. Sauté until softened.
  5. Sprinkle cumin and paprika, stirring well to coat evenly.
  6. Add rinsed lentils and vegetable broth to the pot.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 30-40 minutes until lentils are tender.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.
