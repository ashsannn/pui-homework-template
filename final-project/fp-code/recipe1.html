Ecuadorian Lentil Soup with 30g Protein

3 servings

300 calories/serving

serving size: 1 cup

50 mins | prep: 15 mins cook: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 3 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 4 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Rinse lentils thoroughly under running water.
  • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.
  • Add the diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, and tomato to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.
  • Sprinkle the cumin powder and paprika over the vegetables and stir well to coat them evenly.
  • Add the rinsed lentils to the pot, followed by the vegetable broth.
  • Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  • Serve the lentil soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
