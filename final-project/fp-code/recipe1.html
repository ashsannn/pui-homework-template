Ecuadorian Lentil Soup with 30g Protein

3 servings

300 calories/serving

serving size: 1 cup

50 mins | prep: 15 mins cook: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 2 tomatoes, diced

  • 3 bell pepper, diced

  • 2 tsp cumin

  • 4 tsp paprika

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • fresh cilanto, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse lentils thoroughly under running water

  2. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.

  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until they become translucent and fragrant.

  4. Add the diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, and tomato to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  5. Sprinkle the cumin powder and paprika over the vegetables and stir well to coat them evenly.

  6. Add the rinsed lentils to the pot, followed by the vegetable broth.

  7. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.

  8. Season the soup with salt and pepper according to your taste.

  9. Serve the lentil soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
